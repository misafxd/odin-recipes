Beef burritos

There's nothing like a tasty beef burrito to fulfill that craving for Mexican cuisineâ€”and these simple, hearty beef and bean burritos are no exception.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shredded cooked beef
  • 1 cup from one can Traditional Refried Beans
  • 8 flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. In two 1-quart saucepans, heat beef and refried beans separately over medium heat 2 to 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until hot. Warm tortillas as directed on bag.
  2. Place about 1/4 cup of the beef on center of each tortilla. Spoon about 2 tablespoons beans onto beef. Top with 1/4 cup of the lettuce, 3 tablespoons tomatoes and 2 tablespoons cheese.
  3. Fold one end of tortilla up about 1 inch over filling; fold right and left sides over folded end, overlapping. Fold remaining end down.
